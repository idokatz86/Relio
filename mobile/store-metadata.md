### Description (4000 chars max)
Love isn't broken. It's just waiting to be rediscovered.

Relio is an AI-powered communication tool for couples. When emotions run high and the right words won't come, Relio sits between you and your partner as a neutral third party — translating frustration into understanding.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Express yourself privately: Share what you really feel in Tier 1 — your raw thoughts that stay completely confidential
2. AI translates: Our mediator transforms your message into constructive, Socratic questions your partner can hear
3. Your partner receives the constructive version: Only the de-escalated Tier 3 message reaches them

THE 3-TIER PRIVACY MODEL
Your private venting is physically separated from shared conversations. This isn't a policy — it's architecture. No admin, no partner, no one can see your Tier 1 private thoughts. Ever.

FEATURES
• Real-time AI mediation between both partners
• Private journal for venting (encrypted, never shared)
• Attachment style assessment (anxious/avoidant/secure)
• Communication pattern tracking
• Educational resources based on Gottman Method and EFT
• Safety detection for crisis situations
• Emergency resources with locale-aware hotlines
• Available in English, Spanish, Portuguese, and Hebrew
• Biometric lock (FaceID/TouchID) for privacy

IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER
Relio is NOT therapy, counseling, or medical advice. It is a communication tool that helps couples express themselves more constructively. If you are in crisis, please contact emergency services or a licensed professional.

PRIVACY FIRST
• EU data residency (Sweden)
• GDPR compliant (data export + deletion)
• End-to-end encryption
• No data sold to advertisers — ever
