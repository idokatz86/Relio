The Privacy Architecture

Privacy is architecture,
not policy.

Every competitor can write a privacy policy. We built a system where your private words physically cannot reach your partner. Not by promise — by design.

Tier 1 — Private

Never shared

Your raw venting, complaints, accusations, and private journals. Stored in an isolated database partition with no network path to your partner's data. No API endpoint, no SQL JOIN, no admin override.

  • Isolated database partition per user
  • Separate network subnet — no peering
  • End-to-end encryption at rest and in transit
  • Safety Guardian has read-only monitor access
Abstraction Layer
Names, quotes, and specific language are stripped. Only patterns remain.

Tier 2 — Abstracted

Internal only

Clinical pattern-level insights: "Anxious attachment activated," "Criticism pattern detected," "Pursuit-withdrawal cycle." No raw words, no names, no quotes.

  • Pattern-level psychological insights
  • Stored in separate clinical database
  • Used only by the Medical Pod AI agents
  • Never surfaced directly to either partner
Validation Layer
Chief Psychology Officer audits every output before delivery.

Tier 3 — Actionable

Safe for partner

Socratic questions that surface the real emotional need — without revealing what was said privately. Validated by the Chief Psychology Officer before delivery.

  • Constructive Socratic questions
  • Validated by meta-audit agent (CPO)
  • Surfaces needs, not accusations
  • Delivered to the shared mediation room

FAQ

Common questions.

Is Relio therapy?

No. Relio is structured AI mediation, not therapy. We don't diagnose, treat, or prescribe. We use evidence-based clinical frameworks (Gottman Method, Emotionally Focused Therapy, Adult Attachment Theory) to transform how partners communicate. Think of Relio as a skilled translator between two people who love each other but have lost the ability to be heard.

Can my partner see what I type privately?

Absolutely not — and this isn't just a promise, it's architecture. Your Tier 1 private data is stored in an isolated database partition with no network path to your partner's data. There is no API endpoint, no database JOIN, and no admin override that could surface your private words. The AI transforms your raw feelings into constructive Socratic questions before anything reaches the shared space.

What if only one partner wants to use Relio?

Relio delivers genuine value even solo — private journaling, 1-on-1 AI coaching, attachment style profiling, and psychoeducation. When your partner is ready to join, their first experience is absolute privacy, not a sales pitch. We solve the "reluctant partner" problem through architecture and experience, not pressure.

What happens in an emergency?

Our Safety Guardian agent continuously monitors for signs of coercive control, domestic violence, and self-harm. It holds absolute veto power over all other agents. If a critical safety signal is detected, the dedicated Emergency Response Agent can route to real emergency services (911/112/999). Safety features are never paywalled — they're available on every tier, including Free.

How is this different from couples therapy apps?

Existing apps offer exercises (Lasting), quizzes (Paired), or human therapists at $65–100/week (BetterHelp). Nobody mediates live conversations between two people with an AI that holds secrets. Relio's 3-Tier Confidentiality Model — where private data physically cannot be accessed by the other partner — is a category-defining innovation that can't be replicated by bolting a chatbot onto an existing app.

Do you sell my data?

Never. "Revenue Never Degrades Privacy" is one of our non-negotiable principles. No selling data. No "upgrade to see what your partner said." No paywalling safety features. Our core thesis is that privacy-architecture is the moat — compromising it would destroy the very thing that makes Relio work.
