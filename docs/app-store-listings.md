# Relio — App Store Listings (4 Languages)

## English (US)

**Title:** Relio — AI Relationship Mediation  
**Subtitle:** Communicate better with your partner  
**Category:** Health & Fitness > Mental Health

**Description:**
Relio is an AI-powered communication tool for couples. Instead of texting things you'll regret, Relio's AI rewrites your messages into constructive, Socratic questions — so your partner hears your needs without the attack.

How it works:
• You write what you really feel (Tier 1 — Private)
• AI rewrites it into a constructive question (Tier 3 — Shared)
• Your partner sees only the kind version

Built on decades of research from Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT) and the Gottman Method.

**Keywords:** relationship, couples, communication, mediation, AI, therapy alternative, conflict resolution, couples app, dating, marriage
