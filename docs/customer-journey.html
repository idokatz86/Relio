Safety Escalation
Safety Guardian · Emergency Response · CLO · CPsychO
🚨

SAFETY_HALT Triggered

Pipeline stops. No Tier 3 output. Partner sees: "Your partner is taking a moment. We're ensuring everyone is safe."

⚠️

HIGH Severity

Localized crisis resources: US (988, DV hotline, 911) · Brazil (CVV 188, SAMU 192) · Israel (ERAN 1201). Compassionate AI messaging.

🔴

CRITICAL Severity

Route to 911/112/999. CLO notified within 5 minutes (duty-to-warn). Session locked — human clinical reviewer required. Post-crisis follow-ups: 24h, 72h, 7 days.
