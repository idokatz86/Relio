Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 26, 2026 | Version 1.0.0

1. What Relio Is

Relio is an AI-powered relationship mediation app. It uses artificial intelligence to help couples communicate better. Relio is NOT therapy, counseling, or a medical service.

2. The 3-Tier Confidentiality Model

Relio operates on a strict 3-tier data separation model:

  • Tier 1 (Private): Your raw messages. These are ONLY visible to you. They are never shared with your partner, stored on our servers, or used for training.
  • Tier 2 (Abstracted): Pattern-level insights generated by AI (e.g., "anxious attachment detected"). These are internal-only and never shown to users.
  • Tier 3 (Actionable): The AI-mediated, de-escalated guidance shown to both partners in the shared room. This is the only content visible to your partner.

3. Data We Collect

  • Account info: Email address (via Clerk OIDC), user ID.
  • Messages: Processed in-memory for AI mediation. Raw Tier 1 messages are NOT stored on our servers.
  • Device info: Crash reports (via Sentry, PII-scrubbed), device locale for language selection.
  • Subscription: Managed by RevenueCat. We do not store payment details.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Location data
  • Contact lists
  • Photos or media
  • Browsing history
  • Your partner's private journal entries

5. On-Device Storage

Journal entries and profile data are encrypted using your device's Secure Enclave (iOS) or Android Keystore. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly request account deletion.

6. AI Processing

Messages are processed by Azure OpenAI GPT-4.1 models. We do NOT use your data to train AI models. Our Azure OpenAI deployment has data processing agreements that prohibit model training on customer data.

7. Account Deletion

You can request account deletion at any time from Settings → Delete All Data. Deletion includes a 24-hour grace period, after which all data (Tier 1, 2, 3, consent records, invite codes) is permanently purged.

8. Children

Relio is restricted to users 18 years and older. We verify age during onboarding and do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Third-Party Services

  • Clerk: Authentication (OIDC). Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management. Privacy Policy
  • Sentry: Crash reporting (PII scrubbed). Privacy Policy
  • Azure OpenAI: AI processing. Privacy Policy

For privacy inquiries: privacy@myrelio.io

11. Changes

We will notify users of material changes via in-app notification and update the version number above.
