Trust by Design

Privacy isn't a policy. It's our architecture.

Every competitor can write a privacy policy. We built a system where your private words physically cannot reach your partner — enforced by network isolation, separate databases, and cryptographic barriers.

🔒
Tier 1 — Private

Your Eyes Only

Raw venting, complaints, and private thoughts. Stored in an isolated database with no API endpoint to surface this content. Not even Relio's admins can read it.

  • Separate database per user
  • No cross-tier database JOINs
  • Network-level isolation
🧠
Tier 2 — Abstracted

Pattern Intelligence

Clinical insights like "anxious attachment activated" or "flooding detected." Used by Relio's AI internally to craft the right response. Never shared with your partner.

  • AI-internal analysis only
  • Encrypted at rest
  • Clinical-grade data handling
🛡️
Tier 3 — Shared

Safe to Share

Socratic questions and constructive dialogue crafted by Relio. This is the only content your partner ever sees — validated by a meta-audit AI before delivery.

  • Audited before delivery
  • No original phrasing
  • Canary-tested in CI/CD
